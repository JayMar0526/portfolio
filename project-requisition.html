REQUISITION SYTEM

The web-based application facilitates the processing of requested items or services, generating reports, monitoring request statuses, and providing email notifications regarding request statuses.

Project Overview

As an internal web-based application, this system efficiently manages requests for items or services within the company, addressing the challenge of handling a high volume of daily requests, which often lead to file misplacements. It facilitates the entire process from request submission to fulfillment. Capabilities include report generation for data analysis, real-time monitoring of request statuses, and email notifications to keep stakeholders informed about the progress of their requests. This tool significantly improves workflow management, ensuring that requests are promptly attended to and reducing the risk of missing files or overlooked tasks.
